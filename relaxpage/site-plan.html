Content

Home page

Why will people come to our site? At some point in our lives, we all experience moments where we are too overwhelmed, stressed or unmotivated by our daily activities. Sometimes those same feelings cloud our thoughts, our ideas and we don't feel the need to continue with our routine and we let those bad feelings grow. Even when we want to do something to change those feelings we don't know what to do, or who to turn to for help or where to go to feel a little better. We also do not know what resources to go to on the internet. People will come to this website to be redirected to positive thoughts and search for visual and auditory sources of de-stress in order to eliminate or reduce all those feelings of stress as if a close friend is telling you something or making a recommendation. It will be a very familiar environment instead of a professional one so that the user can feel a little more understood. It will be like a small menu of options to help all people of all ages to feel a little better with the resources they prefer to take. What will users get on the page and why would they choose this website over another? There are many online pages or videos that seek to cover that need to help people feel better, but occasionally there are situations in which users do not find the correct resource or simply do not know how to look for it because there is no one to suggest it, for therefore they do not have access to the solution they are looking for. That is why people who enter this page can expect to find a diversity of visual and auditory resources to calm their minds and find some peace in the midst of their frustration in a warm and pleasant way. Users will be able to find some images with motivational phrases, self-care questions, healthy reminders, relaxing music suggestions and even spotify playlist suggestions where there will be music that will help them calm their minds or that they can have with them to do their activities. with a nice atmosphere. You'll also find video tips for dealing with bad feelings and stress. Another facility is that users will not need to provide personal information or start a session within the page to be able to navigate within it. Access will be free for all those who have the desire to enter and navigate within it in search of help. Therefore, it will be easier and faster to access in difficult times. It will also be of great help because the content can help people of all ages to feel better, although it is suggested that young children access it with the help of their parents in case they cannot read. Visitors to the page would go to this website instead of another because of the friendliness of the colors that they will be able to find, the resources offered in an accessible way and available to everyone and because they do not have to have a specific account or worry for remembering passwords or logging in. When a person is overwhelmed the last thing they want is to enter their data, confirm an email or remember passwords! So simple access will be of great help. At the same time, for the resources that will be suggested to you and the way you will receive them. The idea is that the user feels that he is receiving recommendations from someone who values her life and wants to help him calm his bad feelings. The information will be organized in an easy to understand way so that people of all ages can easily navigate it without further frustration. It will be simple but effective and with free resources to achieve its mission.
